SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great easy recipe for to make simple choclate cakes. It uses very common ingredients found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

EQUIPMENT

  • Cake Tin or Muffin Tray
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Eletric Mixer
  • Cooling Rack
  • Fork
  • Weighing Scales
  • Smaller Bowls (to hold ingredients)

INGREDIENTS

  • 65 grams of Coco
  • 2 eggs
  • 250 grams of Butter
  • 125 mls of Milk
  • 125 grams Self Rising Flour
  • 220 grams Caster Sugar
  • Toppings of your choice

PREPARATION

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C (160 C fan) or use gas mark 5
  2. Grease the Cake Tin or muffin Tray
  3. Sift your flour amd cocoa into the mixing bowl
  4. Beat the two eggs with a fork in a separate bowl then add to mixing bowl
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients to the mixing bowl
  6. Mix on high for 4 minuets
  7. Pour the mix into the Cake Tin or Muffin Tray

COOKING INSTRUCTONS

  • Bake for 35-40 minuets for a cake
  • Bake for 12-15 minuets for a cake
  • Put a cake tester tootpick into the cake. If it comes out clean, your cake is done.
  • Place cake or muffins on the cooling tray and wait to cool.

DECORATIONS

Once the cake or cupcake has cooled, decorate them. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana. But you can choose whatever toppings or decorations you like.

ENJOY!
