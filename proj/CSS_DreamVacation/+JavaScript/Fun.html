Precipice - Exteme
Very steep and dangerous, not suited for thoes afraid of heights. Dispute the extramities, Precipice offers an exclusive cliffside experience like no other. [Closed in fall for falcon nesting]


Beehive - Hard
The shorter version of Precipice. Open year round, each season with their own breath taking veiws. Hike at your own caution.


Ocean Path - Easy
A flat trail on the edge of the island from Sandy Beach to Otter Point and passes by many points of attraction. Suited for family strolls and morning walks.


Cadillac Summit Loop - Easy
Like Ocean Path, but there is no paved or graveled path. Flat rocks and perfect for a short walk.


South Bubble Trail - Moderate
A trail around the top of Jordan Pond, it passes by bubble rock, a unique landmark, and can be quite breath taking to behold.


Bar Island - Easy
Only availible during low tide, this trail is the small stretch from Bar Island to it's neiboring island. here your could see crabs scuttle beneath your feet and look for clams and starfish.

Acadian meals tend to be more seafood centered, with bluberries (on season). It is truly one of a kind.
