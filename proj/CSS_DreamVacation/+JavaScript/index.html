~Arcadia National Park~


A peaceful, close to nature, yet still equiped with elements from modern society vacation spot. Here, the world is of your taking. A plentiful wildlife, teaming with that of differnt species of animals most natural scenaries, and a splendid array of hiking trails, and attractions.
Arcadia National Park is one of a kind. Located in Maine, US, it sits roughly 100 miles from the US/Canadian border. By that statement, it is to assume that Arcadia experiences rather colder weathers. Aside from such Arcadia is quite a beauty to visit. Not only does it exhibit all four seasons, but all in it's own grace and beauty. Arcadia could also be known for it's abundant wildlife. Not only does Acadia hold abundance in mammals birds, forestry, and so many more, it is also a habitat, with it's natural and mostly untouched natural sceneries to go along with. Arcadia's tide pools are quite facinating, along ceritain areas, visitors are also allowed to pick up, and learn about the ocean-life. Arcadia Borders a small town also known as Bar Harbor. Even if it is a rather small town, others of such size cannot compete with its hustle-and-bustle, its life and dynamics. Arcadia is also known for its lobster. Seeing how Arcdia, borders the sea, is no exception to Maine's renown lobsters. Arcadia is also known for it's wild blueberries which (at the right season) you can find in abundance.
